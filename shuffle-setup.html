Switch from Google to User/Pass

BettrBot needs an email & password login to connect to Shuffle.us. If you signed up with Google, follow these steps to add a password to your account.

1
Log in to Shuffle.us
Go to shuffle.us and sign in with your Google account like you normally would.
2
Open Settings
Click your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select Settings from the dropdown menu.
3
Go to Security
In the Settings page, find and click the Security tab.
Settings › Security
4
Unlink your Google account
Find the Google linked account section and click to unlink it. This removes Google as your login method.
⚠️
Make sure you set a password (next step) before logging out, or you won't be able to sign back in.
5
Click "Change Password"
On the same Security page, click Change Password to set a new password for your account.
6
Enter & confirm your new password
Type your new password, confirm it, and save. You can now log in to Shuffle.us with your email and this password.
✅

You're all set

Open BettrBot, select Shuffle.us, and click User/Pass to log in with your email and new password.
