Connect to Shuffle.us

BettrBot connects to Shuffle.us using a Bearer token from your browser session. Follow these steps to grab your token and paste it into BettrBot.

1
Log in to Shuffle.us
Open shuffle.us in your browser and sign in to your account like you normally would.
2
Open DevTools
With Shuffle.us open, press F12 or right-click anywhere on the page and choose Inspect. A panel will open on the side or bottom of the browser.
3
Go to the Network tab
At the top of DevTools, click Network. If the list looks empty, that's fine — it will fill up as you play.
💡
Keep DevTools open for the next steps. If you close it, you'll need to repeat this.
4
Open Blackjack and place a bet
Navigate to Blackjack on Shuffle.us, set any bet amount, and click Play. This triggers a request that contains your Bearer token.
5
Find the graphql request
In the Network tab, scroll through the list and click the entry named graphql. A side panel with request details will pop out.
Network › graphql › Headers
6
Copy the Bearer token
In the Headers tab, scroll down to Request Headers and find authorization: Bearer .... Copy the long string after the word Bearer — that's your token.
7
Paste into BettrBot
Open BettrBot, select Shuffle.us, and paste the token into the Bearer Token field. Click Get User Agent ↗ to grab your User Agent too, then hit Connect.
