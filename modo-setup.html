Modo.us Setup

How to Connect

Follow these steps to get your Bearer token from Modo.us and connect it to BettrBot.

1

Open Modo.us and Log In

Go to modo.us in your browser and log into your account. Make sure you're on the main site (not in the app).

2

Open a Blackjack Game

Navigate to Games and open any Blackjack table. You need to be on the actual game page.

3

Open Developer Tools

Press F12 on your keyboard (or Ctrl + Shift + I on Windows, Cmd + Option + I on Mac) to open DevTools.

Click the Network tab at the top of the DevTools panel.

4

Play One Hand Manually

Place a bet and play one hand of Blackjack in the browser.

üí° You only need to make one action.
5

Find the Bearer Token

In the Network tab, click on Fetch/XHR to filter the requests. Look for a request to play or pick.

Click on it, then go to the Headers tab. Scroll down to Request Headers and find the line that says:

Authorization: Bearer eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIs...

Copy everything after "Bearer " ‚Äî that entire long string is your token.

6

Paste into BettrBot

Open BettrBot, paste the token into the Bearer Token field, and click Connect. Your balance will appear and you're ready to play!
